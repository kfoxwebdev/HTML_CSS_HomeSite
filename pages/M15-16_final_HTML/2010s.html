    Nyan Cat

    This highly popular video was first posted in 2011 and features a catchy chiptune song with a looping video of a pixelated poptart-cat flying through space on a rainbow. Produced in the early stages of "internet meme culture," the video quickly spawned spinoff-videos (like Nyan Cat's evil-twin TacNayn) and even real-world merchandise. At the time of this posting, Nyan Cat has over 203 million views.
